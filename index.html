Calendar Integration

Connect your Google calendar directly to your Team Slot account. You can manage your personal Google Calendar directly from Team Slot in real time. Team Slot also provides a design-efficient interface that makes scheduling a painless task.

Create a Team

After your login with your Google account and integrate your calendar, you can create a new team and invite potential team memebers via mail.

Overapping Team Calendars

Calendar events of all team members will be overlapped under a single calendar. This allows the user to observe time slots in which all the team members are free.

Creating Team Events

After finding a mutually free time slot, the user can create an event that will be automatically added to all of the team member's personal and team calendars.

Features That Everyone Will Enjoy

  • Flexibility

    Team Slot introduces a diverse scope of features, ranging from Team Scheduling (for large groups) and Scheduling Links (for one-to-one meetings).

  • Location Integration

    When a user clicks on a team event, he will be redirected to a Google Maps page containing the location of the event.
