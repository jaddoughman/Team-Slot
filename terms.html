Using our Services

Last Modified: May 04,2018

Google’s API terms of service helped us mitigate risks by enforcing the following consequences.

If a user creates more than 10,000 events on their calendar within a relatively short period of time, then that user may lose calendar edit access.

Additionally, if a user, creates more than 25 new calendars with a short period of time, or shares one or multiple calendars with multiple other users within a short period of time, then that user’s calendar will go into read-only mode.

Furthermore, Google helps us prevent users from spamming our application by limiting the numbers of invitations that a user can send to guests to 100-300 guests.

We can reduce the risk of team members leaving our project by making sure that are properly informed of what they are signing up for. We made sure that each of our members were given tasks that they were the most comfortable with in order to reduce their stress levels as much as possible. Doing this reduces the risk of disputes arising and members leaving. We always strive for maintaining a healthy environment for the sake of our project, and most importantly, our well-being.
